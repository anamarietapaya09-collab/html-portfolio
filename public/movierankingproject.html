The Best Movies According to Ana Marie

My top 3 movies of all-time

3 Idiots

3 Idiots is one of my favorites because it’s funny, emotional, and inspiring all at once. It reminds me to follow what I love, not just what society expects. Every time I watch it, I learn something new and end up feeling motivated.

Life is Beautiful

Life is Beautiful is one of my favorite movies because it blends humor, love, and tragedy in such a powerful way. The story shows how far a parent will go to protect their child, even in the worst circumstances. Guido’s positivity and imagination turn a terrifying reality into something bearable for his son, and that message stays with you long after the movie ends. It is emotional, inspiring, and a reminder that love can create light even in the darkest places.

The Boy in the Striped Pajamas

The Boy in the Striped Pajamas is one of my favorites because it shows the innocence of childhood in the middle of a cruel world. The friendship between Bruno and Shmuel is pure and heartbreaking, reminding us how hatred is taught, not born. The ending stays with you — it is tragic, powerful, and makes you reflect on compassion, ignorance, and the real cost of prejudice.
